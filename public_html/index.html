Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

What types of construction services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive construction services including structural engineering, electrical and plumbing work, POP installations, tiling, interior and exterior design, industrial cleaning, stucco application, and aluminum window installations.

Do you provide cost estimates for projects?

Yes, we provide detailed cost estimates for all projects. Our team conducts thorough assessments to ensure accurate pricing that fits various budgets while maintaining our quality standards.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 3-12 months, while commercial projects may take 6-24 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely! We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee and provide comprehensive warranties on all construction and design projects. Specific warranty terms depend on the type of work performed.

Can you work within my budget?

Yes! We pride ourselves on offering cost-effective solutions for varying budgets. Our team works closely with clients to design and implement projects that meet their financial requirements without compromising on quality.
