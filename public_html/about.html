About RH Global Innovation

Leading construction and design excellence across Nigeria with integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction

Our Story

RH Global Innovation Nig Ltd was founded with a clear vision: to transform the Nigerian construction landscape through innovative solutions, quality craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

Led by our visionary CEO, Silas Stephen Oyaluna, our company has grown from humble beginnings to become a trusted name in the construction and design industry across Lagos and Nigeria.

We understand that every building project represents dreams, aspirations, and investments. That's why we approach each project with dedication, professionalism, and attention to detail that exceeds expectations.

Our journey has been marked by successful projects, satisfied clients, and continuous growth in our capabilities and expertise.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving excellence in construction through our core purpose and future aspirations

Our Mission

Transforming visions into reality with affordable, quality construction solutions.

We are committed to delivering exceptional construction and design services that meet our clients' needs while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and professionalism. Our goal is to make quality construction accessible to all segments of society.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria's most trusted and innovative construction company.

We envision a future where RH Global Innovation is synonymous with excellence, reliability, and innovation in the Nigerian construction industry. We aim to set new standards and inspire positive change in how buildings are designed and constructed.

Our Leadership

Silas Stephen Oyaluna

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Silas Stephen Oyaluna is the visionary founder and CEO of RH Global Innovation Nig Ltd. With extensive experience in the construction industry, Mr. Oyaluna has built the company from the ground up with a commitment to excellence and innovation.

Under his leadership, RH Global Innovation has completed numerous successful projects across Lagos and Nigeria, earning a reputation for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. His passion for transforming the Nigerian construction landscape drives the company's continued growth and success.

Mr. Oyaluna believes in empowering his team, investing in cutting-edge technology, and maintaining the highest standards of professionalism in all business dealings. His vision continues to guide the company toward becoming Nigeria's most trusted construction partner.
