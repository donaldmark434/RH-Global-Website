About RH Global

Building excellence across Nigeria with integrity, innovation, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Story

RH Global Innovation Nig Ltd was founded with a vision to transform the construction landscape in Nigeria. Starting as a small construction company in Lagos, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in building construction and design services.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every construction project deserves exceptional quality, innovative solutions, and transparent communication. This philosophy has guided us through countless successful projects and established our reputation for excellence.

Through strategic partnerships, including our collaboration with Mekan Engineering, we have expanded our capabilities and enhanced our service offerings to provide comprehensive construction solutions.
